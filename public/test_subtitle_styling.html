Usage Instructions

In Video Player:

  • Click the 🎨 button in the video control bar to cycle through preset styles
  • Use window.videoPlayer.applySubtitleStyling('large bold outline') in console
  • Use window.videoPlayer.setSubtitleStyling({size: 'large', theme: 'blue'}) for custom styling

Available Options:

  • Sizes: default, large, small
  • Weights: default, bold
  • Styles: default, italic, underline
  • Effects: default, outline, glow
  • Themes: default, dark, light, blue, green, red, yellow
  • Positions: default, top, left, right
  • Animations: default, fade-in, fade-out, slide-up, slide-down
